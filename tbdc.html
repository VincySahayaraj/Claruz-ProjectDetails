TBDC, founded in 1990, is one of Toronto's pioneering business incubators. With three decades of experience, TBDC has been instrumental in fostering entrepreneurship, supporting startups, and facilitating business growth across Ontario. As a not-for-profit multi-sector incubator, TBDC plays a vital role in bridging the gap between global entrepreneurs and Ontario's investors, industry associations, and government bodies.

Problem

TBDC faced several critical challenges with its current website. First, the website is inundated with excessive information, creating a cluttered and confusing user experience. Second, the user interface and experience were outdated, hindering user engagement and interaction. Third, there was a significant absence of a well-defined customer journey, leaving visitors without a clear path to explore TBDC's extensive resources and services.

The Brief

Addressing the challenges through a website revamp was imperative to ensure that TBDC remained a leading force in supporting and nurturing the business community. They wanted a website that presented information in a concise, organized, and user-friendly manner, develop a visually appealing and intuitive UI with an improved UX, and design a customer journey that guides visitors seamlessly through TBDC's services, fostering a more purposeful and engaging user experience.

Our Strategy

To address the multifaceted website challenges, we devised a comprehensive and impactful strategy. We decided to declutter and reorganize the website to make it super easy for users to find what they need. No more information overload – just clear, concise, and accessible content. But that's not all; the real magic lied in the customer-centric journey which made the website not only informative but also visually appealing and engaging.
