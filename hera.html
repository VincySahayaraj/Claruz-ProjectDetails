Problem

Hera's existing website was developed using Umso, a template-based website launching platform. This choice led to a site that lacked the unique design essence and creativity that Hera wanted to convey. The templated nature of the website didn't allow Hera to showcase its unique approach to fertility care, making it difficult to stand out in a competitive market. The website didn't fully encapsulate Hera's mission of providing a digital-first, accessible, and affordable platform for fertility care.The site lacked clear pathways for potential users to engage with Hera's services, such as joining a waitlist for the Hera Clinic or booking virtual consultations with fertility advisors.

The Brief

Hera aimed to establish itself as the go-to destination for all fertility care and needs. The objective was to offer consumers a comprehensive experience where they could learn, understand, and place their trust in the brand for their fertility journey. The tone of voice on the website was designed to be warm, comfortable, and convey a sense of trust. The imagery chosen encapsulated themes of family, fertility, birth, and care.

Our Strategy

In our discussions with the Hera team, understanding their brand narrative, diving deep into consumer research, and exploring various mood board variations, we identified the essential attributes that would lead to an engaging web design and an enriching customer journey. We chose to emphasize the warmth, trust, and comfort inherent in the fertility journey, transforming these feelings into visual design elements while always prioritizing the user experience.
